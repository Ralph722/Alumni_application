================================================================================
                            RESPONSIVE BEHAVIOR
================================================================================

Desktop (1200px+):
  - Full layout as shown above
  - Sidebar: 200px fixed
  - Form: 30% of content
  - Table: 70% of content

Tablet (768px - 1199px):
  - Sidebar may collapse to icons
  - Form and table stack vertically
  - Adjusted padding and spacing

Mobile (< 768px):
  - Sidebar becomes hamburger menu
  - Full-width form and table
  - Single column layout
  - Adjusted font sizes
